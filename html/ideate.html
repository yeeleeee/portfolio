Tools Used

  • Github Pages takes care of my hosting. Amazing!
  • Brackets is the best editor. I love how it makes my coding workflow super fast, and you can even customize the colors from different themes.
  • Grammarly - as a non-native speaker, this app is my mentor and buddy.
  • Dynalist is an amazing workflow webapp - you don't need to download it, just type the address and you are good to go!
